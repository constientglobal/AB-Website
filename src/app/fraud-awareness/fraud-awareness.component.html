Online Fraud Awareness

Your Security is Our Priority

At Absolute Broking Private Limited, we are committed to providing you with a secure and reliable platform for all your financial transactions. In the digital age, staying vigilant against online fraud is crucial. We urge all our clients to be aware of common fraud tactics and take necessary precautions to protect their investments and personal information.

Key Security Measures We Employ

Robust Encryption: All data transmitted through our platforms is secured with advanced encryption technologies.

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): We encourage and support MFA to add an extra layer of security to your accounts.

Regular Security Audits: Our systems undergo continuous monitoring and regular security audits to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities.

How You Can Protect Yourself

Be Suspicious of Unsolicited Communications: We will never ask for your password or OTP via email, SMS, or phone.

Verify Sender Identity: Fraudsters often use similar-looking addresses. Always double-check authenticity.

Never Share Credentials: Do not disclose your username, password, PIN, or OTP to anyone claiming to be from Absolute Broking.

Use Strong, Unique Passwords: Combine letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid repeating passwords across sites.

Beware of Phishing Links: Never click on suspicious links. Type the official website URL directly into your browser.

Regularly Monitor Your Accounts: Stay on top of your transaction history. Report suspicious activity immediately.

Keep Your Software Updated: Update your operating system, browser, and antivirus regularly for protection.

Report Suspicious Activity: If you believe your account is compromised, contact Absolute Broking immediately via official support.
