Filing Complaints on SCORES – Easy, Transparent & Efficient

The SEBI Complaints Redress System (SCORES) is an empowering initiative by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) designed to provide investors with a simple, transparent, and effective means to raise their concerns and grievances related to India's capital markets. SCORES offers a centralized online platform where investors can lodge complaints against listed companies and SEBI-registered intermediaries in a user-friendly and completely digital environment. To initiate a complaint, investors must first register on the | SCORES | portal by visiting https://scores.gov.in, where they can create an account using a valid email ID and mobile number. During registration, it is mandatory to provide key details including the full name as per PAN, PAN number, registered address, mobile number, and a valid email ID. Once the registration process is complete, investors can log in to the portal to describe their grievance in detail, attach any relevant supporting documents, and submit the complaint directly to the concerned intermediary or listed company. SCORES not only ensures the efficiency of the redress process through time-bound resolution mechanisms but also offers complete transparency, allowing complainants to track the real-time status of their complaints. It acts as a direct channel of communication between the investor, the involved entity, and SEBI’s regulatory oversight, ensuring accountability at every step. Additionally, the platform is entirely paperless, secure, and convenient, eliminating the need for physical documentation and visits. By leveraging technology, SCORES significantly enhances investor protection and reinforces trust in the financial markets by ensuring grievances are addressed promptly and fairly.
