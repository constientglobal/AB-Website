About Us

Our mission is to seamlessly bridge the gap between finance and technology, creating an efficient ecosystem for diverse financial transactions. We offer a comprehensive suite of services including Equity, Commodity, and Currency broking, along with the distribution of Mutual Funds (MF), Portfolio Management Services (PMS), Alternative Investment Funds (AIF), Fixed Income products, Loans, and Insurance. We cater to both retail and HNI clients through our B2C and B2B channels.


Starting operations from PMG's Shridevi Park Office, ABPL quickly gained significant momentum. We secured AMFI registration, SEBI registration as a stock broker, and obtained membership with BSE, NSE, and NSDL as a Depository Participant. Driven by increasing demand, we swiftly transitioned to a dedicated corporate office in T Nagar, Chennai, and expanded our team with dedicated professionals.


ABPL is deeply committed to digital transformation within the Indian financial sector. Our dedication to innovation and a strong customer-centric approach is establishing us as a trailblazer. We are continuously exploring advanced technologies, expanding our product offerings, and redefining how individuals and businesses engage with financial services. Our journey, though brief, powerfully illustrates the evolving impact of technology on India's financial landscape.
