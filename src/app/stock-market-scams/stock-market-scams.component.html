Beware of Stock Market Scams

Protect Your Investments

At Absolute Broking Private Limited, we are committed to safeguarding your financial interests. While the stock market offers immense opportunities, it's also a target for fraudsters. Being aware of common stock market scams and taking preventive measures is crucial to protect your hard-earned money.

Common Stock Market Scams to Watch Out For:

"Pump and Dump" Schemes: Fraudsters artificially inflate the price of a low-value stock (the "pump") through false and misleading positive statements, then sell their own cheaply acquired shares at the inflated price (the "dump"), leaving unsuspecting investors with worthless stock.

Ponzi/Pyramid Schemes: These schemes promise high returns with little or no risk, paying early investors with money taken from later investors. They collapse when new money stops coming in.

Phishing and Impersonation: Scammers impersonate legitimate financial institutions or advisors through fake emails, websites, or calls to trick you into revealing personal information, login credentials, or transferring funds.

Unsolicited "Hot Tips": Be wary of unsolicited calls, emails, or social media messages offering "guaranteed" high returns or "insider information" on specific stocks. These are often traps.

Fake Investment Products: Fraudsters may promote non-existent or unregistered investment products, often with complex terms, to lure investors.

Advance Fee Scams: You might be asked to pay an upfront fee for a "special" investment opportunity, only for the promised returns or investment to never materialize.

How to Protect Yourself:

Verify Everything: Always verify the credentials of any person or firm offering investment advice or opportunities. Check their registration with regulatory bodies (e.g., SEBI in India).

Do Your Own Research: Never invest based solely on unsolicited advice. Always research the company, its financials, and the market conditions yourself.

Be Skeptical of "Guaranteed" Returns: All investments carry risk. Be extremely suspicious of anyone promising high, guaranteed returns with no risk.

Protect Your Personal Information: Never share your passwords, OTPs, or sensitive financial details with anyone. Absolute Broking will never ask for these.

Use Official Channels: Only interact with Absolute Broking Private Limited through our official website, verified mobile apps, and official contact numbers.

Monitor Your Accounts: Regularly check your trading and demat account statements for any unauthorized transactions.

Report Suspicious Activity: If you encounter any suspicious activity, receive unusual communications, or suspect fraud, report it immediately to Absolute Broking Private Limited and the relevant regulatory authorities.

Your vigilance is your first line of defense. Invest wisely, invest securely.
